Textual Summary of Queries


Reasons for case and their contributions :

The trend is about the reasons for case. It shows the specific reasons like work, family, mental health-related cases. The trends are displayed based on the years and month. Users can adjust the years and month of which they want the data. The data displayed is in the form of visualizations which have reasons and their contribution in terms of percentage. The visualization is in terms of two bar graphs, each representing a year, reasons and their total case time. Year is represented as x-axis and the case time is represented as y-axis.

Hospital with the highest total case due to operational issues :

The trend is about the hospital with the longest total case in a particular case due to economic issues. Users can select the year of which they would like to get the data. The data is displayed in terms of visualization and it contains the name of the hospital which has the longest total case and the total case of the hospital in each month of that year. The visualization is a line graph which has month as x-axis and total_case_time in thousands as y-axis.

Case due to bad work conditions in a city :

The trend is about the case due to bad work conditions in each month of a city in a particular selected year. Users can select the city and year of which they want to get the trend analysis. The trend is displayed in terms of visualization. Visualization contains the name of the hospital which is affected by the work and the case due to work in each month and it also contains the reasons for bad work like wind, storm, snow and fog and their total duration. The visualization has two graphs, one has the months as x-axis and total case time in thousands as y-axis and the other graph has the reasons of bad work which has reasons as x-axis and their contributions as y-axis.

Cities with highest cases during each season :

The trend is about the cities that experience the highest cases in terms of time during each season. Users can select the season for which they want to get the visualization. The trend is displayed in terms of visualization. Visualization contains the name of the top five cities which have highest cases in a particular season. Visualization is in the form of a bar graph. The graph has the city as x-axis and case time as y-axis.

Hospitals with highest number of cases due to family :

The trend displays the top five hospital names that experience the highest number of cases due to family in a particular month of a year. Users can select the year and month for which they want the visualization. Visualization contains the name of the top five hospitals with the most number of cases in a particular year and month. The visualization has a bar graph in which the city represents the x-axis and total number of cases represents the y-axis.
